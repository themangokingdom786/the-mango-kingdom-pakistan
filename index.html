Our Story

Pakistan produces some of the world's finest mangoes and exports premium-quality fruit every year.

We believed local mango lovers deserved the same export-quality experience.

That's why we created premium packaging and a direct farm-to-home delivery system, bringing carefully selected export-grade mangoes straight to your doorstep.

Today, The Mango Kingdom proudly serves customers across Pakistan with fresh handpicked mangoes from Multan.

Chaunsa Mango Guide

Chaunsa Mango is one of Pakistan's most famous mango varieties. Known for its rich sweetness, juicy pulp and exceptional aroma, it is loved by mango enthusiasts around the world.
